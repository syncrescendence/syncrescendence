**Status**: live-v1

## 2. Governing Constraint

Perplexity is a research accelerant.
It is not a constitutional reasoner, not a drafting surface, and not a creation engine.

Its lawful value is concentrated in:

- discovery
- reconnaissance
- source mapping
- current-reality sensing
- citation-scaffolded verification

Downstream synthesis, doctrine, code, and durable artifacts belong elsewhere unless a later surface-specific contract says otherwise.

## 3. Durable Harness Advantages

The imported witness identifies four durable advantages that matter to the shell:

1. domain-corpus routing through Focus modes
2. inline citation at claim level
3. Deep Research for cold-start domain reconnaissance
4. Model Council as structured disagreement surfacing, with shared-index caveats

For Syncrescendence, the most important conclusion is not "Perplexity is better."
It is that Perplexity is better only for a narrow class of research tasks.

## 4. Augur Placement

Augur is the shell's research reconnaissance and verification avatar.

Its lawful role is:

- scan the current source terrain
- find the primary or near-primary documents faster
- surface disagreements or current product reality
- return a cited witness set that stronger reasoning surfaces can audit

Its unlawful role is:

- writing final doctrine
- acting as constitutional authority on repo-local state
- producing polished final artifacts by default
- replacing primary-source checking on high-stakes claims

## 5. Research-Routing Law

The imported teleology implies an asymmetric routing law:

- use Perplexity early for reconnaissance, source discovery, and current-reality mapping
- use stronger reading or reasoning surfaces afterward for synthesis, critique, or artifact production
- use repo-native evidence whenever the question is about the shell's own state

That means Perplexity should normally sit:

- upstream of synthesis
- adjacent to verification
- outside final authorship

## 6. Syncrescendence Interpretation

Inside this shell, Perplexity should be treated as a narrow exocortical sensing and verification surface.

Its best use cases are:

- cold-start market or capability reconnaissance
- official-doc or product-surface current-state checks
- source-set generation for later repo compaction
- promoted-item verification sidecars

Its weaker use cases are:

- open-ended architectural authorship
- polished writing
- code generation
- long-lived memory authority

## 7. Acumen Implication

Perplexity should not become the core Acumen ingestion or triage engine.

Acumen's mainline remains:

- YouTube polling
- triage
- deterministic or intelligent processing
- Dawn Brief and downstream evidence

Perplexity becomes relevant after triage, mainly when an item is:

- promoted
- flagged for primary
- or otherwise worthy of broader source-map expansion

In that position, Augur can enrich a promoted video with:

- source discovery
- current-reality verification
- competitor or context reconnaissance
- citation-grounded follow-up packets

## 8. Deferred Surface Rule

Perplexity Spaces and other provider-specific project surfaces remain deferred.

Until the shell formalizes cross-frontier project-feature rendering, the correct move is:

- preserve the exact witness
- compact its durable logic into Sigma
- use packetized or playbook-driven Perplexity interactions where already lawful
