# Chat App Custom Instruction Architecture v1

**Status**: live-v1  
**Class**: sigma doctrine  
**Purpose**: define how vendor-hosted chat-app custom instructions fit into the Syncrescendence authority stack

## 1. What These Artifacts Are

Chat-app custom instructions are surface-specific behavioral constitutions for vendor-hosted chat interfaces.

They are the compression layer that adapts stable shell doctrine, operator profile, and anti-pattern law into the exact constraint surface of a provider UI:

- ChatGPT's split two-field form
- Claude's large user-preferences block
- Grok's global plus agent partitioning
- Gemini's bifurcated web-app snippets and AI Studio system instruction

They are not the sovereign source of truth.
They are rendered behavioral veneers for surfaces that cannot ingest the whole shell directly.

## 2. Authority Placement

The lawful ordering is:

- canon: the bind-on-default macro and domain doctrine
- Sigma: repeated-use secondary doctrine about how these surfaces should be shaped
- chat-app custom instructions: provider-specific renderings of that doctrine
- playbooks: surface-specific operational doctrine once the surface has stable workflow law

The exact imported CI texts belong in [knowledge/sigma/references/chat-app-custom-instructions](/Users/system/syncrescendence/knowledge/sigma/references/chat-app-custom-instructions) because they are source-proximate reference substrates.
Their reusable logic belongs here in Sigma.

## 3. Cross-Surface Invariants

The imported provenance report establishes a stable six-part invariant set that should travel across vendor surfaces unless a platform's constraint surface makes a specific element impossible:

1. response scaling must match complexity rather than imagined brevity preferences
2. observation, inference, and speculation should be separated and labeled
3. prose should be active, affirmative, and direct
4. recurring anti-patterns should be explicitly suppressed
5. the model should collaborate through honest pushback rather than servile agreement
6. the operator's cognitive profile should be accommodated structurally rather than cosmetically

These are the load-bearing patterns.
The exact wording varies by platform because the steering surfaces vary.

## 4. Platform-Specific Packaging Rules

### 4.1 ChatGPT

Treat the two fields as a compression problem.
Field 1 carries the behavioral contract.
Field 2 carries only the identity and cognitive-profile scaffold that recurrently changes behavior.

### 4.2 Claude

Treat the large user-preferences field as a constitutional room, not as permission to bloat.
Claude can hold a richer XML architecture, but the correct move is still surgical high-yield instruction rather than decorative verbosity.

### 4.3 Grok

Treat Grok as a decomposed architecture.
The global layer should hold the enduring behavioral foundation.
Named agents should amplify specific mission types rather than restate the whole constitution repeatedly.

### 4.4 Gemini

Treat Gemini as bifurcated.
The web app wants light behavioral nudges that do not trigger persona over-performance.
AI Studio can absorb the fuller synthesis architecture.

## 5. Guardrails

- do not mistake provider-surface CI text for shell canon
- do not let platform memory or vendor UX become hidden sovereign law
- do not collapse API prompting guidance, per-task output contracts, and persistent chat-app instructions into one artifact
- do not preserve only the rendered surface and lose the rationale that produced it
- do not rewrite these surfaces ad hoc without preserving provenance and cross-platform parity checks

## 6. Maintenance Rule

The durable asset is the revision methodology, not merely the current wording.

The preserved provenance report records the working cycle:

1. triangulated ground-truth acquisition
2. bridge analysis that translates vendor mechanics into usable CI design
3. constitutional drafting inside the target surface's actual constraints

When a provider surface changes materially, the shell should rerun that cycle rather than patch the old text blindly.

The shell now carries:

- exact current copies of the canonical ChatGPT, Claude, Grok, and Gemini custom-instruction texts
- the provenance report explaining why they look the way they do
- a live Sigma doctrine surface that locates them inside the authority stack

This means future edits can preserve both behavioral fidelity and lineage instead of relying on memory or scattered Desktop files.
