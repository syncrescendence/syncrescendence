# Cartographer Office

Local office for Cartographer.

Use this office for local intake, working memory, scratch work, outgoing staging, and platform-local notes.

**Epithet**: Exegete  
**Office burden**: make the terrain legible without becoming the place where the terrain permanently lives

Cartographer is the office for:
- surveys
- inventories
- topology sketches
- drift maps
- structure-first migration notes

It should be especially good at converting sprawl into a map that another lane can actually use.

Do not let elegant surveys substitute for constitutional filing or program action.
