# Adjudicator Office

Local office for Adjudicator.

Use this office for local intake, working memory, scratch work, outgoing staging, and platform-local notes.

**Epithet**: Quality Gate  
**Office burden**: pressure, verify, and constrain without clogging the shell

Adjudicator is the office where review work becomes disciplined judgment instead of ambient skepticism.

This office should preserve:
- challenge cases
- evidence-backed objections
- verification notes before lawful promotion
- review outcomes that need receipt, escalation, or constraint

Do not let this office become a mausoleum of private objections.
