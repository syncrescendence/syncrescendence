# Knowledge Lane Law v1

## Purpose

`knowledge/` exists because the predecessor shells repeatedly carried a distinct research and feedstock subsystem that cannot be reduced to pedigree, runtime, or validated patterns without losing semantic resolution.

The successor shell uses `knowledge/` for living domain knowledge that is neither:

- constitutional law,
- executive direction,
- program sequencing,
- communications lineage,
- nor raw runtime state.

## Internal Sub-Lanes

- `knowledge/references/`: distilled, traversable research substrates used by playbooks, design work, and future compaction.
- `knowledge/canon/`: domain-level durable knowledge that has been promoted beyond mere references, but is still distinct from constitutional shell law.
- `knowledge/feedstock/`: lawful intake for incoming source bundles, raw research drops, and pre-compaction material.

## Distinctions

- `pedigree/` preserves ancestry and predecessor evidence.
- `knowledge/` holds living research and domain cognition used by the current shell.
- `validated-patterns/` holds already-proven operators and operating patterns.
- `communications/` holds prompts, responses, handoffs, and assessments, not topic knowledge.

## Intake Rule

New research should land in `knowledge/feedstock/` or `knowledge/references/` first, then be compacted upward into:

- `knowledge/canon/`
- `playbooks/`
- `validated-patterns/`
- or shell law

as appropriate.

## Initial Occupant

The first explicit occupant is the distilled archived `neocorpus`, internalized under `knowledge/references/neocorpus/` as a living reference substrate.
