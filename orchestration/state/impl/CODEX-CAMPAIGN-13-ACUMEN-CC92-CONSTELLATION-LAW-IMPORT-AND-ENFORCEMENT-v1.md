# Codex Campaign 13 — Acumen CC92 Constellation Law, Import, And Enforcement v1

**Status**: staged  
**Class**: swarm macro campaign scaffold  
**Purpose**: turn the five-account chain ecology into a lawful inbound feed system for Acumen before any large browser-bound or platform-account execution

## Why This Campaign Exists

Campaign 12 landed the first real post-triage product family, but it did not land the upstream widening system.

The next real gap is not another derivative product.
It is the governed conversion of curated chain accounts into an inbound source ecology that can widen Acumen without fragmenting intake sovereignty.

The attached canon makes the direction clearer:

1. feed curation is cognitive substrate engineering
2. Acumen remains the sovereign intake and triage plane
3. the other four chain accounts are real upstream curation surfaces
4. recon before mutation, source-of-truth capture, and stop-on-ambiguity still hold
5. old account-transfer topology is stale, but its recon discipline is still load-bearing

## Campaign Rule

This campaign should land repo-local law, manifests, validators, importers, and enforcement before pushing into platform-account creation or browser mutation.

Worker sessions should:

1. preserve Acumen as the only active shared raw intake plane
2. treat the five chain accounts as curated upstream reservoirs
3. keep platform state, captured manifests, imported portfolio state, and downstream products as distinct truth classes
4. favor YouTube-first registry compatibility while allowing non-YouTube captures to remain portfolio-visible but registry-deferred
5. leave platform-account creation and full browser execution as a precise next frontier, not as hand-wavy present-tense scope
