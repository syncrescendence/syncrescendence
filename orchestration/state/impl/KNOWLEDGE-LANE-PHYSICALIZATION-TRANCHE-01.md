# Knowledge Lane Physicalization Tranche 01

## Trigger

Archive-fit manifests for the predecessor shells showed a repeated top-level family that the successor shell still lacked:

- `04-SOURCES` / `sources`
- `neocorpus`
- parts of `05-SIGMA`

The shell could not honestly claim lawful lossless retrofit until research/feedstock had a live lane distinct from pedigree.

## Action

This tranche introduces `knowledge/` at the root with:

- `knowledge/references/`
- `knowledge/canon/`
- `knowledge/feedstock/`

and internalizes the archived distilled `neocorpus` into `knowledge/references/neocorpus/`.

## Consequence

The successor shell now has a lawful place for:

- live research substrates
- incoming source material
- future compaction from research into playbooks, patterns, and law

without forcing those materials into pedigree or runtime.
