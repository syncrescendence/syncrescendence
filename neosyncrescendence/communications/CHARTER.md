# Communications Charter

This lane governs explicit inter-surface lineage.

Allowed classes:
- prompts
- responses
- handoffs
- logs
- retros
- assessments

Rules:
- Prompt and response artifacts are peers, not scattered side effects.
- Handoffs move state between operators or agents.
- Logs and retros are continuous-improvement records, not execution outputs.
- Assessments summarize or adjudicate, but do not replace source artifacts.

This lane exists to stop hidden chat state from becoming authority.
